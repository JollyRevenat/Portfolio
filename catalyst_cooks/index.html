About Us

Catalyst Cooks, founded in 2022, is operated entirely by students at the Davis Catalyst Center who aim to sustain the project with the school's assistance. The students manage every aspect of the operation, from recipe development to marketing, and abide by their motto "We Make It, You Bake It." When customers order a meal online, the students assemble the kit, and customers collect their delectable meal to enjoy. Despite their youth, the students receive guidance from industry experts to ensure that they produce the highest quality products.

What is the Davis Catalyst Center

The Davis Catalyst Center is where innovation, collaboration, and education converge. Students at the Catalyst Center engage in real-world, professional experiences by working together and partnering with various organizations. Our program offers diverse pathways for Catalyst students, including computer science, unmanned systems, business marketing, and entrepreneurship. These pathways provide valuable insights into the professional world, allowing students to explore different career options. Our dedicated students exemplify hard work and creativity, consistently delivering remarkable and unique creations.
