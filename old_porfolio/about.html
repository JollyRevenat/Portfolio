About Me

Hey there! I’m Charles Blake, a student at Bountiful High School with a passion for technology and the mountains.

I study Web Development and Cybersecurity at the Catalyst Center, where I’ve been building my skills in front-end design and am currently diving into database development.

On the cybersecurity side, I love exploring networks, servers, and operating systems. I’ve been experimenting with booting persistent OS drives from USBs and learning how computers communicate behind the scenes. Lately, I’ve also been working on a few small AI projects.

I’m graduating at 17 and planning to take a gap year to keep learning hands-on before deciding whether to jump into college or go straight into the tech industry. Either way, I want to keep growing in the world of computers and problem-solving.

Outside of tech, I’m happiest in the mountains. I ski at Snowbasin Mountain Resort, mountain bike, and play lacrosse for Bountiful High. I love music, pickleball, and anything that lets me stay active and outdoors.
