About Me

Hi there! I'm a passionate high school web developer currently working at the Davis Catalyst Center, where I get to turn creative ideas into digital reality every day.

My journey into web development started with curiosity about how websites work, and it quickly became my passion. Just like conquering mountain peaks, I love the challenge of solving complex problems and the satisfaction of seeing my code come to life in the browser.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or collaborating with my team at the Catalyst Center on innovative web solutions. I believe in writing clean, efficient code and creating user experiences that are both functional and beautiful.

Much like preparing for a ski run, I'm always excited to take on new challenges and learn emerging technologies in the ever-evolving world of web development. Every project is a new slope to master!
